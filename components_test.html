Air Brake Certification

Component Encyclopedia

Review these parts before taking the test. These are the core items for the Class 1 / CDL exam.

Air Compressor

Pumps air into the system. Driven by gears or a belt. Must be checked for secure mounting and leaks.

✔ Check: Build rate should be 85 to 100 PSI in 2 minutes or less.

Governor

Controls the compressor's pumping cycle. Cuts-in at ~100 PSI and Cuts-out at ~125 PSI.

✔ Check: Must cut-out between 120 and 135 PSI.

Air Dryer

Removes water and oil from the air before it reaches the tanks to prevent freezing.

✔ Check: Listen for the 'purge' sound when the governor cuts out.

Slack Adjuster

Adjusts the gap between brake shoes and drums. Manual or Automatic.

✔ Check: Pushrod travel should not exceed 1 inch (at 90-100 PSI).
